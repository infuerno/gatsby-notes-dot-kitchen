- Take screenshot: **Shift + Cmd + 3**
- Capture a portion of the screen: **Shift + Cmd + 4**
- Capture a window or menu: **Shift + Cmd + 4 + Spacebar**
- To clipboard instead of file: As above **+ Ctrl**
